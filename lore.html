The Lore of Khael'Voreen

In the time before time, when the Eternal Forest whispered secrets to the stars and the gods still walked among the living, there was a city of stone and starlight: Tonwe Khael’Voreen Et. The Assembly ruled not through power, but remembrance — a solemn duty to preserve the voices of the Ancient Ones.

But peace is a fragile thing. From beyond the ashen veil rose horrors long thought extinguished — twisted remnants of the Ravaging, when gods fell and memories were devoured. The Eternal Forest grew quiet. The pyres of Iss-Hadal roared anew.

Now, a Vanguard stirs — chosen not by blood, but by dream. They are wanderers and warriors, mages and madmen. They carry stories yet unwritten, bound to a fate older than flame. They seek the forgotten.

This is their tale.
