Lore of Khael'Voreen

Long ago, before the rise of empires, the Eternal Forest whispered truths only the brave dared remember...

Through flame, shadow, and sacrifice, the Vanguard now walks the same roads as gods once did.

Their journey begins in Tonwe Khael’Voreen Et — the City of Shadows and Fire. Beneath its stone halls, secrets slumber, waiting to awaken once more...
