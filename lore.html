Lore of Khael'Voreen

Long before the rise of kingdoms and the fall of empires, the lands of Khael'Voreen were shaped by the will of the Ancient Ones...

As magic tore across the skies and eldritch forests whispered secrets to those who dared enter, few survived to record the truth of what came before...

But within the Eternal Forest, and beneath the molten peaks of Karrak, remnants remain. These tales are pieced together by flame and faith, and by those who dare remember...
